Instructions

  • Enter your LinkedIn user id (stored in local storage).
  • Compose your post and save it (stored in local storage).
  • Click the "Copy HTML to Clipboard" button to copy the post content.
  • Click the "Create a new LinkedIn post" link to open the LinkedIn post page.
  • Open the Developer tools (F12) and put the following in the console:
    • var html = `[pasted from clipboard]`
    • $('.ql-editor').innerHTML = html
